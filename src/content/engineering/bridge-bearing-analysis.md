A bridge built in 2010 with a design lifespan of 100 years was already showing failing bearings, more than half of the rubber bearings had displaced significantly.

My work was to **find the cause and propose a fix**, by inspecting the current state of the structure and cross-referencing it with the archive data. Having identified the cause, I wrote a feasibility study setting out the possible solutions.
