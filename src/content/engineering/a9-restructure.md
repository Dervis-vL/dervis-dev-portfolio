As part of the restructuring of the **A9 motorway** in the Netherlands, between Badhoevedorp and Holendrecht, I worked on the calculations for a new viaduct as well as recalculations and strengthening measures for several existing viaducts.

All work was carried out at **Definitive Design** level, in accordance with the European norms and standards.
