One of my first full-stack builds, from a software-development course covering web, desktop and mobile development. The brief: give a restaurant more visibility and show its pizza menu to customers.

The **web application** is a **Django** site with a REST API, styled with HTML and CSS and deployed to Heroku. The **mobile and desktop app** is built in **Python with Kivy**, packaged via Buildozer. Simple, but a satisfying combination of essential tools working together — and an early sign of where my interests were heading. (For where that pizza obsession led, see the [Pizza Platform](/work/pizza-platform).)
